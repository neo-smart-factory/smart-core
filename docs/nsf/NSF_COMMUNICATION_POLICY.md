# NSF Token - Communication Policy

## 🎯 Purpose

This document establishes mandatory communication guidelines for all references to the NSF Token to ensure regulatory compliance and accurate positioning.

**Target Audience**: Marketing team, community managers, developers, partners, investors

**Scope**: All public communications including websites, social media, documentation, press releases, and investor materials

**Enforcement**: ZERO TOLERANCE for violations

---

## ⚖️ Core Positioning Statement

### Mandatory Context

**ALWAYS include this statement when mentioning NSF:**

> "The NSF is an instrument of coordination within the Neural Sync Factory protocol, not a financial asset. It enables qualified access to decentralized tokenization infrastructure without promise or expectation of financial return."

### Short Version (for space-constrained contexts)

> "NSF: Protocol coordination instrument, not an investment."

## 🎓 Educational Content Guidelines

### When Explaining NSF

**DO:**
- ✅ Explain technical functionality (ERC-20, permit, etc.)
- ✅ Describe access qualification process
- ✅ Detail governance parameters
- ✅ Clarify separation of ownership from access rights
- ✅ Emphasize utility-only nature

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Discuss price predictions
- ❌ Suggest holding strategies for profit
- ❌ Compare to other "investment" tokens
- ❌ Imply value appreciation
- ❌ Use case studies about "successful investors"

## 🚨 Disclaimer Requirements

### Mandatory Disclaimer (Website Footer)

```
DISCLAIMER: The NSF token is a protocol coordination instrument and is not 
intended as an investment, security, or financial product. NSF does not 
represent equity, debt, or any form of ownership in NEØ Protocol or its 
affiliates. Holding NSF does not guarantee financial returns, dividends, 
or appreciation. NSF's sole function is to enable qualified access to the 
Neural Sync Factory protocol. Participation involves technical and market 
risks, including potential loss of entire value. This is not financial 
advice. Consult legal and financial advisors before acquiring NSF.
```

### Mandatory Disclaimer (Documentation)

```
REGULATORY NOTICE: NSF is classified as a utility token under Brazilian 
(CVM), US (SEC), and EU (MiCA) frameworks. It is not regulated as a security. 
This classification may change based on evolving regulations or enforcement 
actions. NSF holders are responsible for understanding applicable laws in 
their jurisdiction.
```
